Simple Steps

How MigrantSafe Works

Anyone can use this platform — no technical skills needed. Just follow these 4 simple steps.

🧑‍💼

Create Your Account

Register as a migrant worker or recruitment agent. It's completely free and takes under 2 minutes.

🔍

Search for Agents

Browse our database of recruitment agencies. See their verification status — Verified, Pending, or Reported.

🚨

Report Bad Actors

If an agent cheats or misleads you, fill out a simple report form. Admins review it and update the agent's status.

💬

Ask the Chatbot

Have a question about visas, contracts, or fees? Ask our AI chatbot in English, Malay, or Bangla — anytime.

Report Suspicious Agents

Been cheated, overcharged, or misled? Submit a report. We collect the details and alert the admin. Other workers can see flagged agents before it's too late.


Your identity stays protected — reports are reviewed confidentially.

🌏

Multilingual Interface

The platform works in English, Bahasa Melayu, and বাংলা (Bangla). Switch languages with one click — no need to struggle with unfamiliar words.

🔐

Secure Login & Roles

Three types of accounts — Migrant Worker, Recruitment Agent, and Admin. Each role sees only what is relevant to them. Your password is encrypted and safe.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Admins can view all reports, update agent statuses, manage user accounts, and keep the platform clean and trustworthy — from a simple, organised dashboard.

Our Mission

Aligned with UN SDG 8

MigrantSafe directly supports Target 8.8 of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals — protecting the rights of all workers, including migrant workers, and promoting safe working environments. By reducing exploitation, improving transparency, and giving workers access to verified information, we work toward a more just and ethical labour market in Malaysia.
